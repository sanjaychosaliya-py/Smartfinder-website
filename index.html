About SmartFinder

Built by students,
for students.

SmartFinder was born in 2023 inside a cramped hostel room in Surat. We were frustrated — endless WhatsApp forwards, unreliable brokers, no transparency. So we built the solution ourselves.

Our platform connects students across UTU, SVNIT, VNSGU and more with verified, comfortable PGs. No broker fees. No hidden charges. Just smart, simple living.
